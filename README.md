# Al Rawda Water Production Company  
## Production, Sales & Operations Dashboard (Power BI)

---

## Project Summary
This project delivers an **interactive Power BI dashboard** for **Al Rawda Water Production Company**, a UAE-based bottled water producer.  
The dashboard provides actionable insights into **production efficiency, sales performance, and operational costs** to support data-driven business decisions.

The analysis follows a **story-based structure**, moving from overall performance to detailed insights and clear recommendations.

---

## Company Overview
**Al Rawda Water Production Company** is a UAE-based manufacturer and distributor of bottled drinking water, serving commercial and institutional customers across the UAE.

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Home
- Company overview
- High-level KPIs
- Page navigation using bookmarks

---

### 2. Production Analysis
Analyzes manufacturing performance and resource efficiency:
- Total production and good output
- Production by product type
- Production and downtime by production line
- Shift-wise efficiency comparison
- Energy and water consumption trends
- Waste rate and production efficiency

**Objective:**  
Evaluate productivity, quality, efficiency, and sustainability.

---

### 3. Sales Analysis
Examines revenue and profitability drivers:
- Sales and profit trends over time
- Revenue by product type
- Units dispatched by warehouse
- Profit by sales channel
- Revenue by dispatch region
- Key financial KPIs

**Objective:**  
Identify top-performing products, channels, regions, and warehouses.

---

### 4. Operations Performance
Focuses on cost control and operational efficiency:
- Temperature vs waste rate analysis
- Logistics cost by transport mode
- Cost by product type
- Downtime vs operational cost by production line
- Energy-intensive production lines
- Quality loss cost impact

**Objective:**  
Detect inefficiencies, cost drivers, and optimization opportunities.

---

### 5. Insights & Recommendations
Consolidates insights from all analyses and provides strategic actions:
- High production efficiency with low waste
- Revenue and profit concentration risk
- Energy and logistics cost optimization opportunities
- Sustainability improvement areas

---

## Key Recommendations
- Optimize energy usage on high-cost production lines  
- Reduce dependency on a single product and sales channel  
- Improve water efficiency and waste control  
- Strengthen underperforming regions and digital sales channels  

---

## Expected Business Impact
- Improved operational efficiency and cost control  
- Reduced business risk through diversified revenue streams  
- Enhanced sustainability performance  
- More balanced and resilient business growth
